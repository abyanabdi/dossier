CASE FILE 002 / ACTIVE INVESTIGATION

The AI Voice Incident

A digital investigation into artificial intelligence, voice impersonation, and advanced social engineering attacks.

Case Classification

Status Active Investigation
Category AI Fraud
Difficulty Intermediate
Evidence Files 4

Case Brief

A corporate employee receives an urgent phone call from someone claiming to be a senior company executive requesting an immediate financial transfer.

The caller's voice appears identical to the executive. The conversation includes internal company information, creating the appearance of a legitimate request.

After the transaction is completed, the executive denies making the call. Investigators are assigned to determine how the deception occurred.

Investigators must determine: Was this a legitimate request, a compromised account, or an AI-generated voice impersonation attack?

Your task is to analyze audio evidence, communication records, and digital traces to identify whether artificial intelligence was used in the attack.

Investigation Timeline

8:42 AM: Employee receives an urgent phone call requesting a financial transfer.

8:49 AM: Follow-up email confirms the requested transaction.

9:15 AM: Financial transfer is approved and completed.

11:30 AM: Executive reports that they never made the request.

2:00 PM: Investigators begin analyzing digital evidence.

Investigator Objectives

Your mission is to determine whether artificial intelligence was used to impersonate an employee and manipulate the victim.

Investigators must:

  • Identify indicators of AI-generated voice manipulation.
  • Analyze communication patterns and digital evidence.
  • Evaluate whether company information was compromised.
  • Determine the social engineering techniques used.
  • Develop recommendations to prevent future attacks.
