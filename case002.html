CASE FILE 002

The AI Voice Incident

An investigation into artificial intelligence, voice impersonation, and modern social engineering attacks.

Status Active Investigation
Category AI Fraud
Difficulty Intermediate

Incident Overview

A financial employee receives an urgent phone call from someone claiming to be the company's Chief Financial Officer. The caller's voice sounds identical to the executive and provides specific internal information about an upcoming transaction.

Following company procedures, the employee completes the requested transfer. Hours later, the executive denies making the call. Investigators must determine whether the incident involved AI-generated voice manipulation, account compromise, or another form of deception.

Investigation Brief

Incident Type

AI-Assisted Social Engineering Attack

Target

Corporate Financial Department

Primary Question

Was the voice authentic or artificially generated?
