Email Header & Phishing Analysis

Investigate whether the recovered communication represents a legitimate executive request or a targeted social engineering attack.

Investigation Objective

Your role is to examine the recovered email as a digital investigator. You must evaluate sender authenticity, communication patterns, and manipulation techniques before reaching a conclusion.

Remember: A suspicious indicator does not automatically prove criminal activity. Investigators must analyze multiple sources of evidence before making a determination.

Reply-To Investigation

The sender claims to represent an organization. However, responses are redirected to:

Michael.Reynolds.finance@gmail.com

Finding: The reply address does not match the organization's official email infrastructure.

Criminological Analysis

Authority Manipulation

The offender uses executive identity to increase victim compliance. This exploits workplace hierarchy and trust.

Urgency Creation

Time pressure reduces opportunity for verification and encourages rapid decision-making.

Isolation Strategy

The request to avoid discussing the payment prevents social verification from coworkers.
