STAGE 01

Incident Briefing

Review the complaint and identify the first responsible investigative step.

Emma Chen, a 21-year-old computer science student, applied for a remote internship advertised on a professional networking platform. A recruiter calling himself Ryan Cole conducted a text-based interview and sent an offer from ryan.cole@northstar-digitalcareers.com.


Emma received a cheque for $3,200 and was told to purchase a laptop and software from an approved vendor. Her bank made the deposit appear available. She sent $2,450 by e-transfer. Two days later, the cheque was reversed as counterfeit. The recruiter stopped responding and the equipment website disappeared.

Select the first responsible investigative action.
