CASE FILE 002 / EVIDENCE 002-A

Audio Recording Analysis

Investigate the suspected AI-generated voice recording used during the fraudulent financial request.

Evidence Overview

Evidence Type: Audio File

File Format: WAV Recording

Source: Employee phone recording

Status: Under Analysis

Investigator Findings

Audio analysts identified several irregularities within the recording.

  • Unnatural pauses between sentences.
  • Repeated speech patterns.
  • Limited emotional variation.
  • Slight distortion during pronunciation changes.

These indicators suggest the possibility of AI-generated voice manipulation.

Investigation Decision

Does the audio provide evidence of AI voice manipulation?
