CASE FILE 001 / EVIDENCE 001-C

Digital Footprint Analysis

Investigating the online identity and activity connected to the suspected organization.

Recovered Digital Evidence

Investigators analyze the company's online presence to determine whether the organization exists as a legitimate business.

Evidence collected includes:

  • Social media profiles
  • Employee information
  • Website history
  • Online activity patterns

Digital Footprint Findings

FINDING 01

Limited Online History

The organization has very little digital history despite claiming to operate internationally.

FINDING 02

Suspicious Profiles

Employee profiles appear recently created and contain limited information or connections.

FINDING 03

Victim Account Activity

After submitting information, the victim notices unusual login attempts and password reset requests.

Investigator Analysis

A digital footprint can reveal whether an online identity is authentic. Investigators compare online activity, account history, and available records to identify inconsistencies.

The evidence suggests the organization created a false digital identity to appear legitimate.

Investigator Challenge

Which piece of evidence is most useful for verifying whether an online organization is legitimate?

Reveal Answer

A consistent digital presence including verified company information, employee history, and legitimate online activity.
