CASE FILE 001 / EVIDENCE 001-C

Digital Footprint Analysis

Investigating the online identity and activity connected to the suspected organization.

Recovered Digital Evidence

Evidence Type: Digital Identity Investigation

Evidence ID: 001-C

Investigators analyzed online activity connected to Global Internships Support to determine whether the organization had an authentic digital presence.

Digital Footprint Investigation Report

DIGITAL FLAG 001

Limited Online History

The organization has very little online activity despite claiming to operate as an established international internship provider.

DIGITAL FLAG 002

Suspicious Employee Profiles

Investigators identified profiles with limited history, few connections, and signs of possible fabricated identities.

DIGITAL FLAG 003

Victim Account Activity

After submitting information, the victim reported unusual login attempts and suspicious account notifications.

Investigator Analysis

A digital footprint allows investigators to evaluate whether an online identity is consistent, verifiable, and trustworthy.

The evidence suggests that the organization created a false online identity to gain the victim's trust.

Investigator Challenge

Which evidence would be most useful when verifying whether an online organization is legitimate?

Reveal Investigation Notes

Correct findings:

✓ Verified company information
✓ Consistent employee history
✓ Established online presence
