CASE FILE 001 / CLOSED INVESTIGATION

The Internship Scam

A digital investigation examining social engineering, fraudulent recruitment, and online deception.

Case Information

Status Closed
Category Cybercrime
Difficulty Beginner

Case Overview

A university student receives an internship opportunity from an organization that appears legitimate. After submitting personal information, unusual activity begins appearing across their online accounts.

Investigators must analyze digital evidence to determine whether the opportunity is authentic or part of a social engineering attack.

Investigation Timeline

Day 1: Victim receives an internship offer through email.

Day 2: Victim submits personal information through an external application form.

Day 3: Suspicious account activity appears across the victim's online accounts.

Day 4: Investigators begin analyzing digital evidence and communication patterns.
