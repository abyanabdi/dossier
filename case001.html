CASE FILE 001 / ACTIVE INVESTIGATION

The Internship Scam

A digital investigation into fraudulent recruitment, social engineering tactics, and online deception.

Case Classification

Status Open Investigation
Category Cybercrime
Difficulty Beginner
Evidence Files 3

Case Brief

A university student receives an internship offer from an organization that appears legitimate. The opportunity includes professional messaging, an application process, and requests for personal information.

After submitting information, the victim notices unusual activity across their online accounts. Investigators must determine whether the internship opportunity is authentic or part of a social engineering attack.

Investigation Timeline

Day 1: Victim receives internship offer through email.

Day 2: Victim provides personal information through an external form.

Day 3: Suspicious account activity is detected.

Day 4: Digital evidence collection begins.

Day 5: Investigators analyze collected evidence.
