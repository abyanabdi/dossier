CASE FILE 001

The Internship Scam

Classification: Cybercrime / Social Engineering

Status: Closed Investigation

Case Summary

A university student receives an internship opportunity from an organization that appears legitimate. After submitting personal information, unusual activity begins appearing across their online accounts.

Investigators must analyze digital evidence to determine whether the opportunity is authentic or part of a social engineering scheme.

Evidence Files

Evidence 01: Email Communication

Analyze sender information, wording, urgency tactics, and requests for personal information.

Evidence 02: Website Investigation

Review website details, domain information, design inconsistencies, and suspicious links.

Evidence 03: Digital Footprint

Examine online activity, social media presence, and identity indicators.

Investigation Questions

1. What signs suggest this internship may be fraudulent?

2. What evidence would investigators collect?

3. What tactics did the offender use?

4. How could the victim have prevented the incident?
