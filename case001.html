DOSSIER ARCHIVE / CASE FILE 001

The Internship Scam

Classification: Cybercrime

Category: Social Engineering

Status: Closed Investigation

Difficulty: Beginner

Case Summary

A university student receives an internship opportunity from an organization that appears legitimate. After submitting personal information, unusual activity begins appearing across their online accounts.

Investigators must analyze digital evidence to determine whether the opportunity is authentic or part of a social engineering scheme.
