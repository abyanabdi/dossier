ABOUT DOSSIER

Building the Future of Investigation Education.

Dossier is a student-founded educational platform exploring digital investigations, cybersecurity, forensic science, and artificial intelligence.

Our Mission

Modern investigations are changing. Evidence is no longer limited to physical locations. Emails, digital footprints, online behaviour, and artificial intelligence are transforming how investigators understand crime.

Dossier was created to make these concepts more accessible through interactive case simulations, research resources, and evidence-based learning.

What We Explore

Digital Forensics

Understanding how digital evidence is collected, analyzed, and interpreted.

Cybercrime

Exploring online threats, fraud, and criminal behaviour in digital environments.

Artificial Intelligence

Examining how AI is changing investigations, security, and crime prevention.
