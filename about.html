ABOUT DOSSIER

The Future of Investigation Education

Dossier is an interactive investigation platform designed to explore digital evidence, cybercrime, forensic science, and emerging technologies through realistic case-based learning.

What is Dossier?

Dossier is a digital investigation education platform that transforms complex concepts in criminology, cybersecurity, and forensic science into interactive learning experiences.

Instead of only reading about cybercrime, investigations, and evidence analysis, users step into the role of an investigator by reviewing evidence, identifying patterns, and making evidence-based conclusions.

Through simulated investigations, users learn how modern investigators approach cases involving digital deception, online threats, identity crimes, and emerging technologies.

Why Dossier Was Created

The nature of crime is constantly changing.

Investigations are no longer limited to physical locations, fingerprints, or traditional evidence. Modern investigators must understand emails, websites, digital footprints, artificial intelligence, online behaviour, and large amounts of digital information.

However, many people encounter these topics through technical explanations that can feel difficult or disconnected from real situations.

Dossier was created to make investigation education more accessible by allowing users to experience the process of analyzing evidence and solving cases.

Our Mission

The mission of Dossier is to create engaging, interactive, and realistic learning experiences that help users understand the future of investigations.

By combining criminology, digital forensics, cybersecurity, and emerging technology, Dossier encourages users to develop critical thinking skills and understand how evidence is used to uncover the truth.

The goal is not simply to memorize information. The goal is to develop an investigator mindset: questioning information, recognizing patterns, and making conclusions based on evidence.

How Dossier Works

STEP 01

Investigate Cases

Users enter realistic case files involving cybercrime, fraud, digital identity, and online deception.

STEP 02

Analyze Evidence

Users examine digital evidence including emails, websites, online activity, and investigative findings.

STEP 03

Complete Assessments

Users test their investigative reasoning through questions, scoring systems, and case outcomes.

What Dossier Explores

Digital Forensics

Understanding how investigators collect, preserve, and interpret digital evidence from devices, accounts, and online sources.

Cybercrime Investigation

Examining online fraud, phishing attacks, social engineering, identity theft, and evolving digital threats.

Artificial Intelligence

Exploring how AI is changing both criminal methods and the tools investigators use to analyze evidence.

Human Behaviour & Crime

Understanding how trust, manipulation, decision making, and online behaviour influence digital crime.

The Dossier Approach

Every investigation begins with evidence.

Dossier focuses on teaching users to ask important investigative questions:

  • What information can be verified?
  • What evidence suggests suspicious activity?
  • Are there patterns, inconsistencies, or warning signs?
  • What conclusion is supported by the available evidence?

By focusing on investigation rather than simple answers, Dossier helps users understand how professionals approach complex problems.
