ABOUT DOSSIER

Building the Future of Investigation Education.

An interactive learning platform designed to help students understand digital evidence, cybercrime, forensic investigation, and emerging technology.

Why Dossier Exists

Crime is changing.

Investigations are no longer limited to physical evidence found at a location. Today, investigators must analyze emails, online activity, digital identities, artificial intelligence, and massive amounts of digital information.

However, learning these concepts can often feel complex and disconnected from real-world situations.

Dossier was created to bridge that gap by turning digital investigations into interactive experiences where users can analyze evidence, solve cases, and develop investigative thinking.

Our Mission

Dossier's mission is to make modern investigation education more accessible, engaging, and realistic.

Through interactive case files, evidence analysis, research reports, and investigator challenges, Dossier helps users understand how professionals approach digital crime investigations.

The goal is not only to teach information, but to develop the ability to question, analyze, and think like an investigator.

The Investigator Mindset

Every investigation begins with questions.

  • What does the evidence show?
  • Can the information be verified?
  • Are there patterns or inconsistencies?
  • What conclusion is supported by the evidence?

Dossier encourages users to think critically, investigate carefully, and understand the changing world of digital crime.
