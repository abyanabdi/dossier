CASE FILE 002 / EVIDENCE 002-D

Employee Interview Analysis

Review the witness statement and compare it with recovered digital evidence.

Interview Transcript

Investigator: Did the caller sound like the executive?

Employee: Yes. The voice sounded exactly the same. They knew internal details about the company.

Investigator: Did anything about the conversation seem unusual?

Employee: The caller sounded calm, but there were strange pauses. The request also felt more urgent than normal.

Interview Analysis

  • Attacker used authority and urgency to influence the victim.
  • Internal information may have been collected beforehand.
  • Communication style matched social engineering tactics.
  • Voice similarity alone does not confirm identity.

The interview supports the possibility that the victim was manipulated through a combination of AI technology and social engineering.
