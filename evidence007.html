CASE FILE 002 / EVIDENCE 002-D

Employee Interview Analysis

Analyze witness testimony and compare human behaviour with the digital evidence collected throughout the investigation.

Case Update

Investigators have analyzed the audio recording, email communication, and financial transaction records.

The final stage is understanding how the offender manipulated the employee and why the attack was successful.

Recovered Evidence

Evidence Type: Employee Interview Transcript

Source: Recorded Investigator Interview

Status: Under Review

Interview Transcript

Investigator:
Can you explain what happened on the day of the transaction?

Employee:
I received an email that appeared to come from Michael Reynolds in the executive office. The request seemed unusual because it was marked urgent, but the sender appeared legitimate.

Investigator:
What made you believe the request was authentic?

Employee:
The email included the executive's name and position. Shortly afterward, I received a phone call from someone who sounded exactly like him.

Investigator:
Did you attempt to verify the payment request?

Employee:
Normally I would confirm large payments, but the caller explained the situation was confidential and needed to be completed quickly.

Investigator:
Looking back, do you notice anything unusual?

Employee:
After the transfer was completed, I noticed the email address was slightly different from the company's actual domain.

Behavioral Analysis

Authority Influence

The offender used an executive identity to increase trust and encourage compliance.

Urgency Pressure

The attacker created a time-sensitive situation that reduced careful decision-making.

Isolation Strategy

The employee was discouraged from seeking confirmation from coworkers.

Criminological Interpretation

This evidence demonstrates that cybercrime often involves both technology and human behaviour.

The offender used AI voice technology as a tool to increase credibility, while also exploiting trust, authority, and workplace procedures.

Final Investigator Assessment

Based on the complete evidence file, what best explains why the attack succeeded?
