CASE FILE 001 / EVIDENCE 001-A

Email Communication

Digital Evidence Review

Evidence Summary

Investigators recovered an email sent to the victim containing an internship offer. The email appears professional but contains multiple indicators of fraudulent activity.

Recovered Evidence

Sender:

career@globalinternships-support.com

Subject:

URGENT: Internship Opportunity Selected

Message:

"Congratulations! You have been selected for an exclusive internship opportunity. Please provide your personal information to continue the application process."

Investigator Findings

⚠ Suspicious domain name

⚠ Creates urgency

⚠ Requests personal information

⚠ No verified organization details

Investigation Challenge

Identify three indicators that suggest this email may be part of a social engineering attack.
