CASE FILE 001 / EVIDENCE 001-A

Email Communication

Digital evidence review of a suspected fraudulent internship offer.

Recovered Evidence

Sender:

career@globalinternships-support.com

Subject:

URGENT: Internship Opportunity Selected

Message:

"Congratulations! You have been selected for an exclusive internship opportunity. Please provide your personal information to continue the application process."

Investigator Analysis

⚠ Suspicious Domain

The sender address does not match a verified organization. Fraudulent campaigns often use look-alike domains.

⚠ Urgency Tactics

The message creates pressure by suggesting immediate action is required.

⚠ Information Request

The email requests personal information before providing verified details.

Investigator Challenge

Identify three indicators that suggest this email is part of a social engineering attack.

Reveal Investigation Notes

Correct indicators:

1. Suspicious sender domain
2. Artificial urgency
3. Request for sensitive information
