CASE FILE 001 / EVIDENCE 001-A

Email Communication

Digital evidence review of a suspected fraudulent internship offer.

Recovered Evidence

Evidence Type: Email Communication

Evidence ID: 001-A

Sender:

career@globalinternships-support.com

Subject:

URGENT: Internship Opportunity Selected

Recovered Message:

"Congratulations! You have been selected for an exclusive internship opportunity. Please provide your personal information to continue the application process."

Investigator Evidence Report

Investigators reviewed the email communication and identified several indicators associated with a social engineering attack.

INVESTIGATOR FLAG 001

Suspicious Sender Domain

The sender address uses a domain that cannot be verified as an official organization account.

INVESTIGATOR FLAG 002

Urgency Tactics

The subject line creates pressure by suggesting the opportunity requires immediate action.

INVESTIGATOR FLAG 003

Personal Information Request

The email requests personal information before the organization identity is confirmed.

Investigator Challenge

Which three indicators suggest this email may be part of a social engineering attack?

Reveal Investigation Notes

Correct findings:

✓ Suspicious sender domain
✓ Artificial urgency
✓ Request for personal information
