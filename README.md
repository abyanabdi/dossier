# DOSSIER

## Interactive Digital Investigation Platform

Dossier is an interactive investigation education platform that allows users to explore the world of digital investigations through realistic case simulations, evidence analysis, and investigative challenges.

The platform places users in the role of an investigator, where they examine digital evidence, identify suspicious patterns, evaluate information, and make decisions based on investigative reasoning.

---

# Overview

The way investigations are conducted is changing.

Modern investigators are no longer only analyzing physical evidence. Digital communication, online identities, websites, artificial intelligence, and electronic records have become essential parts of understanding crime.

Dossier explores this changing landscape by creating simulated investigations that demonstrate how evidence is collected, analyzed, and interpreted in the digital age.

Rather than simply learning about cybercrime concepts, users experience the investigative process by reviewing evidence, uncovering indicators, and determining case outcomes.

---

# Mission

Dossier aims to make investigation education more engaging, accessible, and realistic.

The platform is built around the idea that understanding crime requires more than memorizing definitions. Investigators must learn how to question information, recognize patterns, evaluate evidence, and make conclusions supported by facts.

Through interactive cases and research-based learning, Dossier helps users develop the skills needed to understand modern digital threats.
